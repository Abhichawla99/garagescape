Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our garage decluttering services

How much does garage decluttering cost in Calgary?

Garage decluttering starts at $199 for basic decluttering and organization. Full cleanouts with junk removal start at $299. Complete transformations with organization systems are custom-quoted. We offer free, no-obligation quotes — call (587) 804-9266 for an exact price based on your garage's condition.

Do you offer same-day garage cleanout service?

Yes. Whether you're selling your house, dealing with an emergency, or just can't take the clutter anymore, we offer same-day service across Calgary, Airdrie, Okotoks, Cochrane, and Chestermere (subject to availability). Call before noon for the best chance at same-day service.

What do you do with the items you remove?

Usable items are donated to local Calgary charities including Goodwill, Salvation Army, and Women in Need Society. Recyclables go to proper recycling facilities. Only truly unusable items go to landfill — typically less than 20% of what we remove. You'll get a donation receipt for tax purposes.

How long does garage decluttering take?

Most garage decluttering projects take 4-8 hours and finish in one day. Larger or extremely cluttered garages may take 2 days. Complete transformations with flooring and organization systems typically take 2-3 days. We provide an accurate timeline during your free consultation.

Do I need to be present during the decluttering?

We recommend being present at the start so we can understand what to keep versus remove. After that, you're free to leave. Many clients go to work and come home to a fully transformed garage. We send progress photos while you're away.

Are you insured and licensed?

Yes. We carry $2 million in general liability insurance and are fully bonded. All team members are background-checked Calgary locals. Your property and belongings are fully protected.

When is the best time to clean out a garage in Calgary?

Spring is the best time. As the weather warms up in February and March, it's the ideal opportunity to sort through winter gear, clear the clutter, and organize before summer. We see the highest demand from late February through April — book early to secure your spot.

Do you do garage cleanouts near me in Calgary?

Yes. We serve all of Calgary, Airdrie, Okotoks, Cochrane, Chestermere, and surrounding communities. No matter where you are in the greater Calgary area, we can be there same-day or next-day. Call (587) 804-9266 or fill out our form for a free quote.
