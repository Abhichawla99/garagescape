About Brentwood — NW Calgary's Established Gem

Brentwood was developed primarily between the early 1960s and mid-1970s, making it one of the more mature residential communities in NW Calgary. The neighbourhood sits between Crowchild Trail to the east and Shaganappi Trail to the west, with 16 Ave NW (the Trans-Canada Highway) forming its southern edge and the University of Calgary campus just minutes away.

The housing stock reflects that era: mostly bungalows and split-levels on generous lots, with mature elms and ash trees that give the streets a settled, established feel. Market Mall is practically walkable, which is one of the reasons Brentwood has remained popular with University of Calgary faculty, established families, and downsizers who want NW amenities without the new-build premium.

What that history means for garages: the original construction produced either detached single-car structures accessed from back lanes, or single-car attached garages on the side of the main house. Many of these were built to a 10x20 or 12x22 footprint — enough for one vehicle and a workbench, but not much more. Over five or six decades, that space tends to fill up completely.

If you're renovating your Brentwood home — basement development is especially common here as homeowners modernize older spaces — the garage often becomes a temporary staging area that never quite gets returned to order. That's a job we do every week in this neighbourhood.

Common Brentwood Garage Situations We Solve

  • The displaced basement: Reno starts in September, garage fills up with furniture, tools, and boxes, and somehow it's still there the following spring.
  • The inherited accumulation: You bought a Brentwood home and the previous owners left decades of items behind. It's all "probably useful" but nothing has a place.
  • The downsizer situation: Moving from a larger home means items that used to live in a bigger garage now compete for a smaller one. We help prioritize what actually needs to stay.
  • The converted-but-not-finished: Some Brentwood homeowners converted their detached garage to a studio or gym but never properly organized the remaining storage. We create clear zones so both functions work.
  • The four-season sports pile: University area families accumulate gear for every season. We create seasonal rotation systems so you're not digging for ski boots behind the lawnmower in December.

Brentwood Garage Organization — Common Questions

Why are garages in Brentwood so hard to organize?

Brentwood was developed in the 1960s and 70s, which means many homes have detached single-car garages built to the standards of that era — often only 10 to 12 feet wide. These compact spaces accumulate 40 to 50 years of belongings, and homeowners doing basement renovations frequently move overflow storage into the garage temporarily, where it tends to stay permanently. We specialize in making small, packed Brentwood garages genuinely functional again.

Can you help with a garage that has basement reno overflow?

This is one of our most common Brentwood requests. When a basement reno pushes everything into the garage, the car gets displaced and the clutter takes over. We help you sort, donate, and dispose, then install wall-mounted and overhead storage that reclaims the floor for at least one vehicle — even in a compact single-car detached garage.

My Brentwood garage is unheated and detached. What storage systems work?

Unheated detached garages in NW Calgary experience significant temperature swings — from -30°C in January to over 30°C in July. We recommend bins with tight-fitting lids to protect against moisture and pests, steel shelving that won't warp in the heat, and we advise against storing items like paint, batteries, or wine in unheated spaces year-round.

How long does a Brentwood garage organization project take?

Most single-car detached garages in Brentwood that have accumulated years of clutter take one full day — typically 6 to 8 hours. If there is significant basement overflow or decades of accumulated items, we may recommend a two-stage approach: a decluttering session first, followed by storage installation once the scope is clear.

Do you haul away items Brentwood homeowners want to get rid of?

We coordinate donation drop-offs and can arrange disposal for items that can't be donated. Junk removal is not included in the base service price but can be added. We'll give you a clear breakdown during your free quote so there are no surprises on the day.

Do you serve other NW Calgary communities near Brentwood?

Yes. We regularly work in Dalhousie, Varsity, Charleswood, Banff Trail, Montgomery, Bowness, and Hawkwood. If you're in NW Calgary and not sure whether we cover your neighbourhood, just call — we almost certainly do.
