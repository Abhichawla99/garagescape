Our Canmore Garage Organization Process

1

Assessment & Gear Inventory

We walk through your Canmore garage with you, cataloguing what you own by season and sport. Understanding your gear rotation — what you grab weekly versus what hibernates half the year — shapes the entire design.

2

Declutter & Sort

Outgrown kids' skis, retired bikes, duplicate tools, boxes you moved from your last home and never opened — we help you sort everything into keep, donate, sell, and dispose, with calm, judgment-free guidance.

3

Responsible Disposal

Usable sports equipment and household items are routed to donation and consignment wherever possible, and the rest goes to proper recycling and disposal — not the landfill by default.

4

Storage Design & Installation

We install wall-mounted racks, shelving, hooks, and overhead storage matched to your gear inventory — skis vertical, bikes on the wall, bins labelled by season, attractants sealed, and the floor clear for your vehicle.

5

Seasonal Rotation Plan

Canmore life runs on seasons. We set up your garage so the winter-to-summer swap takes twenty minutes, not a weekend: high shelves for off-season gear, prime real estate for what you use right now.

Why Garage Organization Matters More in Canmore

Your Gear Is Your Lifestyle

People move to Canmore for the mountains, and the gear that gets you onto them is a serious investment. Skis delaminate leaning in puddles, bike drivetrains corrode in damp corners, and ropes should never live on a concrete floor. Proper storage protects thousands of dollars of equipment.

Park Inside in Winter

Bow Valley winters are long, and a minus-twenty morning feels very different when your car slept indoors. Most of our Canmore clients start with one goal: get the vehicle back in the garage. We make it happen without giving up gear storage.

Wildlife-Smart Homes

Canmore sits in the middle of a wildlife corridor, and residents are expected to keep attractants secured. An organized garage with sealed storage for garbage, recycling, pet food, and bird seed makes doing the right thing automatic instead of a chore.

Small Spaces, Big Seasons

Canmore real estate is tight, and garages are rarely oversized. Between winter tires, roof boxes, four seasons of sport, and everyday household storage, an unplanned garage fills instantly. Vertical and overhead systems recover space you did not know you had.

Vacation Properties & Rentals

Canmore has many second homes and visitor accommodations. We handle turnover cleanouts and set up simple, guest-proof garage systems so owners always arrive to an organized space — and renters can find the snow shovel.

Moisture & Melt Management

Snow melts off vehicles all winter, and chinook freeze-thaw cycles keep floors wet. Getting cardboard off the slab, gear onto walls, and boots into drying zones prevents mould, rust, and ruined equipment — problems we see in almost every unorganized Canmore garage.

Frequently Asked Questions About Garage Organization in Canmore

How much does garage organization cost in Canmore?

Garage organization in Canmore starts at $199 for a standard garage, with most full projects ranging higher depending on garage size, the volume of gear and clutter, and whether you want shelving or wall systems installed. We provide a free, no-obligation quote before any work begins.

Do you actually travel to Canmore from Calgary?

Yes. Canmore is part of our regular service area — it is about an hour from Calgary on the Trans-Canada Highway. We schedule Bow Valley projects in advance so a full crew arrives with everything needed to finish your garage in one visit whenever possible.

Can you build storage for skis, bikes, and climbing gear?

Absolutely — gear storage is the heart of most Canmore projects. We design wall-mounted bike racks, vertical and horizontal ski and snowboard storage, drying zones for wet gear, bins for climbing and camping equipment, and seasonal rotation systems so summer and winter gear swap places easily.

Does garage organization help with Canmore's wildlife rules?

It helps a lot. Canmore residents are expected to keep wildlife attractants like garbage, recycling, pet food, and bird seed securely stored, and an organized garage makes that far easier. We set up sealed, contained storage zones for attractants so nothing is left accessible or forgotten in a corner.

My Canmore garage is small — can you still help?

Yes. Many Canmore townhomes and duplexes have single or tandem garages that need to hold a vehicle plus a full quiver of mountain gear. Small garages benefit most from professional organization: we use vertical wall systems, ceiling-mounted racks, and overhead platforms to multiply your usable space.

How long does a Canmore garage organization project take?

Most Canmore garages are completed in a single day — typically 4 to 8 hours including decluttering, sorting, and installing storage. Larger projects with custom shelving or heavy cleanouts may take a second visit. We confirm the timeline in your free consultation.
