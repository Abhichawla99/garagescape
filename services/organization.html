Frequently Asked Questions

How long does garage organization take?

Most garage organization projects take 4-8 hours, depending on the size of your garage and the amount of clutter. We'll provide a time estimate during your free consultation.

Do I need to be present during the organization process?

We recommend being present for the initial sorting phase to help make decisions about items. However, we can work around your schedule and can complete most of the work while you're away.

What happens to items I want to get rid of?

We can help arrange donation pickups for usable items and disposal of unwanted items. We work with local Calgary charities and recycling centers.

Will the organization last long-term?

Yes! We create sustainable organization systems with clear labels and logical placement. We also provide maintenance tips to help you keep your garage organized.

Do you provide storage supplies?

Yes, we provide all necessary storage supplies including bins, labels, hooks, and shelving. The cost of supplies is included in our service pricing.

Can you organize a garage that's also used as a workshop?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating multi-functional garage spaces that accommodate both storage and workshop needs, with dedicated zones for different activities.
