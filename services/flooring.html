Frequently Asked Questions

How long does garage flooring installation take?

Most garage flooring installations take 2-4 days, depending on the type of flooring and the size of your garage. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Can you install flooring over existing concrete?

Yes, we can install most flooring options over existing concrete. We'll assess your floor condition and prepare it properly for the new flooring.

How long will my new garage floor last?

With proper maintenance, our garage flooring systems can last 10-20 years or more, depending on usage and care. We provide maintenance instructions with every installation.

Do I need to move my car out of the garage?

Yes, we'll need access to the entire garage floor during installation. We can help coordinate moving your vehicles to ensure the best results.

Can you match my home's interior flooring?

We offer a wide variety of colors and finishes to complement your home's interior design. We'll help you choose the perfect option during your consultation.

Is the flooring safe for children and pets?

Yes, all our flooring materials are safe for children and pets. We use non-toxic, environmentally friendly products that meet all safety standards.
