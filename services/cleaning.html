Frequently Asked Questions

How long does garage cleaning take?

Most garage cleaning projects take 2-6 hours, depending on the size of your garage and the level of dirt and stains. We'll provide a time estimate during your free consultation.

Do I need to move my car out of the garage?

Yes, we'll need access to the entire garage floor for thorough cleaning. We can help coordinate moving your vehicles to ensure the best cleaning results.

Will the cleaning products damage my garage floor?

No, we use professional-grade, floor-safe cleaning products that are designed for concrete and other garage floor surfaces. We never use harsh chemicals that could cause damage.

Can you remove oil stains from my garage floor?

Yes! We specialize in removing stubborn oil stains and automotive fluid marks using professional-grade cleaning techniques and products.

Is the cleaning service eco-friendly?

Absolutely! We use environmentally safe cleaning products that are tough on dirt but gentle on your family, pets, and the environment.

How often should I have my garage professionally cleaned?

We recommend professional garage cleaning every 6-12 months, depending on usage. Heavy-use garages (workshops, frequent parking) may need more frequent cleaning.
