Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom shelving installation take?

Most custom shelving installations take 2-8 hours, depending on the complexity of your system. We'll provide a time estimate during your free consultation.

What materials do you use for shelving?

We use high-quality materials including heavy-duty steel, reinforced wood, and industrial-grade hardware. All materials are chosen for durability and strength.

Can you install shelving around existing items in my garage?

Yes, we can work around existing items and design shelving systems that accommodate your current setup while maximizing available space.

Do you provide a warranty on your shelving systems?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive warranty on all our custom shelving systems, covering both materials and installation workmanship.

Can the shelving be moved or reconfigured later?

Many of our shelving systems are designed to be modular and can be reconfigured as your needs change. We'll discuss your future needs during the design phase.

Do you handle permits for shelving installation?

For most residential shelving installations, permits are not required. However, we'll handle any necessary permits for larger or more complex installations.
