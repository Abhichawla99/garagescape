Our Springbank Hill Garage Transformation Process

1

Design Consultation

We measure your garage, catalogue what needs a home — vehicles, bikes, golf and ski equipment, seasonal tires, tools — and talk through finish level. Springbank Hill garages range from family workhorses to collector spaces, and the design should match how yours is actually used.

2

Declutter & Sort

Before anything is installed, we clear the backlog: outgrown sports gear, duplicate tools, boxes from the last move. Keep, donate, sell, dispose — handled with calm, judgment-free guidance, and usable items routed to donation rather than landfill.

3

Floor First

If your project includes an epoxy garage floor, it goes in before the storage systems — the slab is ground, repaired, and coated while the garage is empty, so cabinets sit on a finished floor instead of around stains and pitting.

4

Cabinets, Slatwall & Overhead Storage

Closed cabinets for chemicals and clutter-prone categories, slatwall for the gear you grab weekly, overhead platforms for seasonal bins and tires. Every zone is planned so vehicle doors open fully and nothing encroaches on parking.

5

Walkthrough & System Handover

We label, walk you through the layout, and leave you with a garage that stays organized because everything has an obvious home — including a plan for the winter-to-summer gear swap.

The Springbank Hill Garage Problem — and How We Solve It

The Last Unfinished Room

Springbank Hill grew fast through the 2000s and is still building out toward 85th Street, which means thousands of beautifully finished homes handed over with completely unfinished garages. Builders leave bare drywall and raw concrete; ten years later, that's often exactly how it still looks — minus the empty floor space. We finish the room the builder didn't.

The Third Bay Trap

A triple garage feels infinite on possession day. Then the third bay absorbs a workbench, patio cushions, two sets of tires, and every box labelled "garage misc." Within a couple of winters a $60,000 vehicle sleeps outside while $600 of clutter sleeps inside. Wall systems and overhead racks reverse that trade permanently.

Active Families, Serious Gear

Between Westside Recreation Centre minutes away, school sports at Rundle and Webber, weekend trips to the mountains on Highway 8, and golf season at nearby courses, Springbank Hill households run deep gear inventories: clubs, hockey bags, skis, bikes for the whole family. Grab-and-go wall storage beats floor piles every time.

Chinooks Are Hard on Concrete

Calgary's freeze-thaw cycling is relentless on the west side's exposed ridge. Meltwater, road salt, and gravel grind into bare slabs every chinook, leaving pitting, dusting, and permanent staining. A coated floor stops the damage and makes winter cleanup a two-minute squeegee job.

Resale and First Impressions

Springbank Hill buyers expect finished spaces, and the garage is one of the first rooms many of them check. An organized garage with a coated floor and built-in cabinets photographs like a feature, not an afterthought — a meaningful edge in an estate-home market.

From Mudroom to Garage, One System

Estate floor plans route daily life through the mudroom — and the garage is the mudroom's overflow valve. We design the two as one system: daily-grab gear at the door, seasonal storage deeper in, so the mudroom stays tidy because the garage actually works.

Frequently Asked Questions About Garage Organization in Springbank Hill

What does luxury garage organization cost in Springbank Hill?

Every Springbank Hill project is quoted individually because estate garages vary so much — a decluttering and organization project starts around $199, while a full transformation with custom cabinets, slatwall, overhead racks, and an epoxy floor is a larger investment scoped to your garage. We always start with a free, no-obligation consultation and a written quote.

Can you organize a triple-car garage without losing a parking bay?

Yes — that is the core of most Springbank Hill projects. Triple-car garages fill up because the third bay quietly becomes a dumping ground. We move storage to the walls and ceiling with cabinets, slatwall, and overhead platforms so all three bays park vehicles again and everything else still has a dedicated home.

Do you install custom garage cabinets in Springbank Hill?

We do. Cabinet systems are designed around your garage's dimensions, your storage inventory, and the finish level you want — from practical powder-coated steel to showroom-grade cabinetry that matches a renovated estate home. We handle design, supply, and installation.

Is epoxy flooring worth it in a Calgary garage?

In Calgary's climate, yes — chinook freeze-thaw cycles drag water, road salt, and gravel onto bare concrete all winter, which pits and dusts the slab over time. A properly prepared epoxy or polyaspartic coating seals the concrete, wipes clean, and gives the garage a bright, finished look that matches the rest of a Springbank Hill home.

We just moved into a new build — when should we organize the garage?

Ideally before the moving boxes settle in. New Springbank Hill builds hand you a bare drywalled garage, and whatever lands on the floor in the first month tends to stay there for years. Organizing early — coating the floor first, then walls and cabinets — means the garage starts finished instead of becoming a project you dread.

Do you also serve Aspen Woods, West Springs, and Discovery Ridge?

Yes. Springbank Hill sits in the middle of our west Calgary service area — we regularly work in Aspen Woods, West Springs, Wentworth, Discovery Ridge, Signal Hill, and out to Elbow Valley and Springbank acreages. One crew covers the whole west side.
