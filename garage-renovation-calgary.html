Types of Garage Renovation: What GarageScape Does and Does Not Do

The word "renovation" means different things to different people. Here is a clear breakdown so you know exactly where GarageScape fits and when to call a general contractor instead.

Organizational Renovation (GarageScape)

This is the transformation of how a garage functions through sorting, storage, and cleaning. No structural changes are required and no permits are needed for this type of work.

  • Full decluttering and sorting
  • Custom storage and shelving installation
  • Deep clean and floor treatment
  • Minor fixture installation (lighting hooks, wall hooks, utility mounts)
  • Seasonal rotation and bin organization systems
  • Floor sealing and epoxy-style coating
  • Overhead ceiling rack installation

Structural Renovation (General Contractor)

This type of renovation changes the physical structure of the garage. This work requires licensed tradespeople and in most cases building permits from the City of Calgary.

  • Electrical wiring or panel work
  • Plumbing installation or modification
  • Drywall and insulation
  • Concrete pouring or grinding
  • Structural wall changes or additions
  • Garage door replacement or framing
  • HVAC installation for heating

Need both? GarageScape and a general contractor can work together. We typically recommend doing structural work first, then calling us to handle the organizational transformation once the space is built out. We can coordinate timing with your contractor.

How an Organizational Renovation Transforms a Calgary Garage

The physical structure of a garage can stay exactly the same and the space can still be completely renovated in terms of how it works. Here is what that transformation looks like.

From Accumulated Clutter to Deliberate Storage

Most Calgary garages are not disorganized because their owners are disorganized. They are disorganized because the garage became the default destination for everything that did not have a home elsewhere in the house. The organizational renovation addresses this by making deliberate decisions about every item and creating a proper home for what stays. The result looks and feels like a different space even though no walls moved.

From Dead Floor Space to Functional Vertical Storage

The biggest missed opportunity in most Calgary garages is the walls and ceiling. Homes in newer Calgary subdivisions often have double-car garages with eight or nine-foot ceilings and completely bare walls. A renovation that installs proper wall-mounted shelving and overhead racks can multiply the effective storage capacity of the garage by two or three times without adding a single square foot of floor space.

From Dirty Concrete to a Proper Finished Floor

Calgary garage floors absorb years of road salt, oil drips, tire marks, and grime. A deep clean followed by a concrete sealer or floor coating makes the floor dramatically easier to maintain and gives the entire space a cleaner, more finished appearance. You do not need new concrete for this transformation. The existing floor just needs to be cleaned properly and protected.

From General Chaos to Zone-Based Logic

A renovated garage has zones: a sports gear zone near the interior door, a tools zone near the workbench, a seasonal storage zone for Christmas and camping, a tire zone. When everything has a zone and a zone has a designated spot, the garage stays organized without ongoing effort. This is the practical outcome of a proper organizational renovation: a system that maintains itself.

Preparing for Your Garage Renovation

A little preparation before GarageScape arrives makes the renovation process faster and the result better. Here is what we recommend.

Move Vehicles Out

Move both vehicles out of the garage the morning of the renovation. We need full access to all floor space and walls. If one bay holds a vehicle that cannot move (under repair, storage vehicle), let us know in advance so we can plan the layout around it.

Be Available for the Sorting Phase

The first few hours are the most important: sorting through accumulated items. Plan to be present or have another adult who knows the household there to make keep/donate/dispose decisions. Trying to sort without anyone present leads to items being set aside that never get decisions made about them.

Know What You Want the Garage to Do

Think ahead about how the renovated garage should work. Do you need to fit two cars? Is there a workshop function? Where do you want sports gear? What seasonal items must stay? The clearer your vision, the better the storage system layout we can design. If you do not have a clear vision, that is fine. We will ask the right questions.

Pre-Sort if You Want to Speed Things Up

If you have obvious items to discard before we arrive (broken tools, expired chemicals, things you already know you do not want), you can pre-sort those into a pile. This is optional but it does speed up the sorting phase if there is significant volume in the garage. We handle all disposal from there.

Working With a Calgary Contractor: What to Do First

If your garage renovation involves both structural work (electrical, insulation, drywall) and organizational work, here is the recommended sequence.

1

Hire the General Contractor First

Structural work always comes first. If you are adding heat, running new electrical, drywalling, or doing any work that changes the physical structure of the garage, those trades need to complete their work before we touch the space. Scheduling our organizational renovation before structural work is finished means we may need to return and redo parts of the job.

2

Clear the Garage Before Structural Work Begins

This is a good time to call GarageScape for a pre-renovation declutter. We can come in before your contractor starts, clear out years of accumulated items, and leave the garage empty and ready for structural work. This is faster and cheaper than trying to work around a full garage, and it means your contractor has clear access from day one.

3

Call GarageScape After the Structural Work Is Done

Once your contractor finishes, call us. We come in, clean up the construction residue, and install the organizational system into the newly finished space. The storage system goes in after drywall and electrical are done so we can properly anchor into studs and use available wall space without working around framing.

4

Tell Both Parties About the Other

If you are coordinating a contractor and GarageScape, let each know about the other. Your contractor may have preferences about what gets installed and where. We may have suggestions about shelving placement relative to electrical panels or windows. Communication between all parties makes the final result better.

Garage Renovation Calgary FAQ

What counts as a garage renovation?

At GarageScape, a garage renovation means fully transforming how the garage functions through sorting, storage installation, cleaning, and floor treatment. We do not do structural work like drywall, electrical, or concrete. For structural changes, you need a licensed contractor. For organizational transformation, we are the right call.

Do you do drywall or electrical work?

No. GarageScape specializes in organizational renovation. We handle storage, cleaning, sorting, and floor treatment. For electrical, plumbing, or drywall, you need a licensed Calgary contractor. We are happy to work alongside a contractor on a multi-phase renovation project.

How long does a garage renovation take?

Small garages can be renovated in two to four hours. Most Calgary double-car garages complete in a full day. Larger or more complex projects may run into a second day. We will give you an accurate time estimate before work begins.

What does a garage renovation cost in Calgary?

Small renovations start at $150-$250. Medium scope (standard double garage) runs $400-$600. Large projects with full deep clean, storage install, and floor treatment run $700-$1,200. Free quotes available on request.

Do you work with Calgary contractors?

Yes. We can coordinate timing with a general contractor so structural work is completed before we handle the organizational side. We have worked on several projects alongside Calgary trades. Let us know about your situation and we will work out a sequence that makes sense.
