The Real Aspen Woods Garage Problem

Aspen Woods grew through the late 1990s and 2000s, attracting Calgary professionals and executives who wanted large lots, proximity to Westhills Towne Centre, and easy Sarcee Trail access to downtown. The homes are substantial and so are the garages. A 24x24 triple garage sounds like a dream — until years of accumulated gear fills it completely.

The COVID years accelerated the problem. Homeowners purchased workshop equipment, home gym gear, and hobby supplies. Those items moved into the garage and never left. Now expensive power tools share space with broken ski bindings, a boat motor waiting for service, and Halloween decorations in boxes no one can reach. Frequently Asked Questions — Garage Organization in Aspen Woods

What makes Aspen Woods garages different from other Calgary neighbourhoods?

Aspen Woods homes feature some of Calgary's largest attached garages — double garages of 22x22 ft are standard, and many properties include triple-car garages of 24x24 ft or more. Epoxy-coated floors are widespread, requiring care during installation. We bring scaled crew sizes and equipment calibrated for these larger footprints every week.

We have an RV and a boat — can you still organize our garage?

Yes, this is one of the most common challenges in Aspen Woods. We zone the garage around your recreational vehicles, design wall and overhead storage to maximize the remaining perimeter, and create a seasonal rotation plan so your boat or RV moves in and out without disturbing the rest of the organization system.

How long does a full garage organization take in Aspen Woods?

A standard double garage typically takes 5–7 hours. Triple garages or heavily cluttered garages commonly take 8–10 hours, sometimes over two visits. We provide a firm time estimate after a free on-site consultation — no surprises on the day.

Do you protect epoxy floors during the work?

Absolutely. We use rubber-padded dollies, felt sliders, and floor protection boards throughout the project. We have never damaged an epoxy floor and take it seriously because replacement costs in Aspen Woods homes are significant.

Do you serve West Springs and Signal Hill too?

Yes. We cover all of SW Calgary including West Springs, Signal Hill, Springbank Hill, Cougar Ridge, and Discovery Ridge with the same fast response time. One call to (587) 804-9266 covers the entire SW quadrant.
