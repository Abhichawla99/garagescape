Makeover vs. Simple Organization: What Is the Difference?

A lot of Calgary homeowners call us asking about organization, but what they actually need is a makeover. The difference matters because it affects the scope, timeline, and cost of the project.

A simple garage organization service assumes your garage is already reasonably tidy and you just need a better system. We come in, design a storage layout, install shelving, and optimize what you have. This works well when the underlying space is already functional.

A garage makeover starts from scratch. It assumes the garage has layers of accumulated items, a floor that has not been properly cleaned in years, and no effective storage system in place. The makeover process clears everything out, cleans down to bare concrete, installs a proper storage system, and treats the floor so the whole space feels new. The result is not a tidier version of what you had. It is a genuinely different space.

For most Calgary families who have lived in their home for more than three or four years, the makeover is the right service. If you are unsure which applies to your situation, call us and we will give you an honest assessment over the phone or during a free on-site visit.

What Is Included in a GarageScape Garage Makeover

Step 1: Full Declutter and Sort

We work through every item in the garage with you. Everything gets sorted into keep, donate, recycle, or dispose. We do not rush this step because the decisions made here determine how well the storage system works afterward. Donated items go to Calgary charities. Recyclables go to City of Calgary facilities. Nothing just gets stuffed into a bin and put back.

  • Full item-by-item sort
  • Calgary charity donation coordination
  • Responsible recycling and disposal
  • Donation summary provided if needed

Step 2: Deep Clean

After the garage is clear, we deep clean the space. Calgary garages accumulate specific types of grime: road salt tracked in from winter roads, oil and grease from vehicles and equipment, dirt from landscaping gear, and in older garages, decades of dust in wall corners and ceiling joists. We address all of it before any storage is installed.

  • Floor sweep, mop, and scrub
  • Oil and road salt stain treatment
  • Wall and ceiling wipe-down
  • Pressure washing available for severe floors

Step 3: Storage System Installation

We design and install a storage system built for how you actually use your garage. This includes wall-mounted shelving, overhead storage if the ceiling height allows, and bin organization systems. We use quality product lines including Gladiator, Fleximounts, and Canadian Tire's Mastercraft line depending on the package and your preferences.

  • Wall-mounted shelving (steel or wood)
  • Overhead ceiling storage racks
  • Labelled bin and container systems
  • Seasonal rotation zones built in

Step 4: Floor Treatment

The final step is treating the floor to protect the concrete and give the garage a clean, finished look. Standard Makeover includes a concrete sealer. Premium Makeover includes an epoxy-style floor coating that resists oil, road salt, and staining. A treated floor is dramatically easier to keep clean and makes the entire garage feel more intentional.

  • Concrete cleaning and prep
  • Concrete sealer (Standard) or epoxy coating (Premium)
  • Oil and salt stain resistance
  • Dried and ready within 24 hours

Calgary Garage Makeover: Real Examples

Every Calgary neighbourhood has its own garage profile. Here are three typical scenarios we see and how the makeover addresses each one.

NW Calgary 2000s Detached Double

The situation: A detached double-car garage in Arbour Lake or Rocky Ridge, built around 2003-2008. Large footprint but low ceilings. Two bikes hanging from hooks that are falling off the drywall. Tires stacked in one corner. Kids' hockey gear in a pile near the door. One car fits if you squeeze past the lawnmower.

After the makeover: Both vehicles fit with walking room. Wall-mounted Gladiator shelving runs the full back wall with zones for sports gear, seasonal items, and tools. Overhead Fleximounts hold the winter tires. The floor has a concrete sealer that makes mopping up salt actually work. The hockey bags hang on a dedicated wall rack by the door.

Typical timeline: One day, two crew members.

SW Calgary Attached Triple

The situation: An attached triple garage in Signal Hill or Springbank Hill. Enormous space that accumulated proportionally enormous amounts of stuff. Two vehicles, a deep freeze, a workbench, landscaping equipment, bikes, and boxes from two previous moves that were never unpacked. One bay is entirely non-functional.

After the makeover: Third bay cleared and transformed into a proper workshop zone with Mastercraft wall panel storage for tools. Main bays reorganized with vertical shelving on all walls. Deep freeze relocated to a practical corner. Landscaping equipment in dedicated overhead racks. Floor treated with full epoxy in the work bay, sealer in the parking bays.

Typical timeline: Two days, three crew members for the larger scope.

NE Calgary Older Single-Car

The situation: An older single-car garage in Rundle or Marlborough, built in the 1970s or 1980s. Tight dimensions. Floor oil stains from decades of use. Original wooden shelving that is sagging and pulling away from the wall. Storage is chaotic but not actually that much stuff -- the problem is the poor use of limited space.

After the makeover: Old shelving removed. Floor deep cleaned and oil stains treated with a degreaser before sealing. New wall-mounted steel shelving goes floor to ceiling on both side walls, dramatically increasing usable storage without taking floor space. Everything the homeowner keeps has a proper spot. Car now fits.

Typical timeline: Half to full day, two crew members.

Makeover Timeline: What to Expect

Most Calgary garage makeovers run on a straightforward schedule. Here is a typical single-day makeover from start to finish.

8:00 AM

Crew Arrival and Assessment

The GarageScape crew arrives and does a quick walk-through with you to confirm the plan and note anything that requires specific handling.

8:30 AM

Declutter and Sort Phase

Everything in the garage is categorized. This typically takes two to three hours for a typical Calgary double-car garage. You make the keep/donate/dispose decisions. We handle the logistics.

11:00 AM

Deep Clean

With the garage clear, we deep clean the floor, walls, and ceiling. Stains are treated. Dust and grime are cleared. The space is ready for installation.

12:30 PM

Storage System Installation

Shelving and storage units go up. This is the longest installation phase. Wall anchors are properly set, units are levelled, and overhead racks (if included) are secured to ceiling joists.

3:30 PM

Floor Treatment

Sealer or epoxy coating is applied to the floor. Drying time varies by product and temperature. In most cases the floor is walkable within four to six hours and vehicle-ready within 24 hours.

4:30 PM

Final Organization and Walkthrough

Items from the keep pile are placed in their designated spots. Everything is labelled. We walk you through the finished system before leaving.

Storage Brands We Use

We work with product lines that hold up in Calgary's climate and can be maintained long-term without special tools or proprietary parts.

Gladiator

Heavy-duty steel wall shelving and storage systems with a modular design. Well-suited to larger garages and homeowners who want to add to the system over time. Holds up well in Alberta temperature swings. Available in steel and laminate finishes.

Fleximounts

Our go-to for overhead ceiling storage racks. Fleximounts are designed for garages, rated for significant weight loads, and adjustable in height. We install these properly into ceiling joists, not just drywall anchors, which is a critical safety difference in an overhead system.

Canadian Tire Mastercraft

The Mastercraft storage line from Canadian Tire is widely available across Calgary, which matters for ongoing maintenance and expansion. We use Mastercraft for wall panels, hooks, and shelving in Standard Makeover packages. Parts are easy to find when you want to add something six months later.

Client-Supplied Products

If you have already purchased shelving or storage units, we will work with what you have. Many Calgary homeowners have Costco shelving units or storage products sitting in boxes. We can install those as part of any package. Just let us know when you call.

Garage Makeover Calgary FAQ

What is included in a garage makeover?

All four steps: full decluttering and sorting, deep clean of the floor and walls, storage system installation (wall shelving, ceiling racks, or both), and floor treatment with concrete sealer or epoxy coating. Both packages include all four steps. The Premium adds upgraded materials and floor coating.

How long does a garage makeover take?

Most Calgary garage makeovers complete in one day. Larger garages or more complex scopes may run into a second day. We give you an honest time estimate before we start, not after.

Do I need to be home during the makeover?

We ask that you or another adult be present for the decluttering phase so keep/donate/dispose decisions can be made. The installation and cleaning phases can proceed without you being there. We will always let you know if we need input.

What about floor prep before the makeover?

Floor preparation is included in both packages. We clean the floor thoroughly, treat oil and salt stains, and apply either a concrete sealer (Standard) or epoxy-style coating (Premium). We do not do structural floor repairs or concrete pouring.

Can you do a garage makeover in Calgary in winter?

Yes. We work year-round. For heated garages, the process is identical to summer. For unheated garages, we use cold-weather-rated products where needed and adjust our approach for temperature requirements. Winter is actually a popular time for makeovers since people have been storing winter gear all season and want a fresh system.
