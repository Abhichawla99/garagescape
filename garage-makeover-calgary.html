Frequently Asked Questions About Complete Garage Makeovers in Calgary

How long does a complete garage makeover take?

Most complete garage makeovers in Calgary take 2-5 days, depending on the scope of work and size of your garage. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do I need to move my car out of the garage?

Yes, we'll need access to the entire garage for the makeover process. We can help coordinate moving your vehicles to ensure the best results.

Can you work around my schedule?

Absolutely! We work around your schedule and can complete most of the work while you're away. We'll coordinate with you for any decisions that need to be made.

What happens to items I want to get rid of?

We handle all disposal responsibly, working with Calgary charities for donations and proper recycling facilities for items that can't be reused.

Do you provide a warranty on the makeover?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on all aspects of your garage makeover, covering materials, installation, and workmanship.

Can you accommodate special requirements?

Yes, we can accommodate special requirements including accessibility needs, specific storage requirements, and unique garage configurations.
