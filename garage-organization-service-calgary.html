Full-Service Garage Organization: What We Actually Do

A GarageScape garage organization service is a complete project handled in a single day by a professional crew. Here is a detailed description of everything that happens from consultation to completion.

Pre-Service: Free Consultation and Quote

Before any work begins, we visit your Calgary garage for a free 30 to 45-minute consultation. We measure the space, document your current storage situation, discuss your priorities and usage patterns, and identify what storage systems would best serve your needs. You receive a written fixed-price quote within 24 hours. The quote is itemized by labour, materials (if any), and any specific add-on services. No work begins until you accept the quote in writing. The consultation is free and creates zero obligation to proceed. This step is what separates an effective organization service from an expensive rearrangement: the plan drives the result, and you see the plan before committing any money.

Service Day: Who Shows Up and When

On service day, a two-person crew arrives at the agreed start time - typically 8 or 9 AM. Both crew members are background-checked employees of GarageScape, not day labour or subcontractors. They arrive with a fully-equipped service vehicle carrying tools, hardware, a basic kit of storage supplies, and any shelving materials specified in the quote. You should plan to be present for the first two hours of the service for sorting decisions, but you do not need to be hands-on for the entire day. Most customers go about their normal day after the sort phase is complete and return to a finished garage in the afternoon.

The Sort: Every Item Gets Decided

The first two to three hours of service are the sort phase. Everything comes out of the garage and is placed on the driveway, sorted into four categories: keep and replace, donate, discard, and decide with homeowner. The "decide with homeowner" category is reviewed with you before anything is removed permanently. Nothing that you want to keep gets donated or discarded. Our crews are trained to ask the right questions - not push you to donate more than you want, but also not to let the discard pile stay small out of excessive caution. An effective sort removes a meaningful volume from the garage. Most Calgary garages give up 20 to 40 percent of their volume during a proper sort. This is the step that creates the space the organization system needs to actually function. See our dedicated garage decluttering service page for what happens to items that leave the garage.

The Clean: Proper Prep for Installation

With the garage fully empty after the sort, we sweep the floor, remove cobwebs from walls and ceiling, wipe down any obvious grime from wall surfaces where shelving will be installed, and assess the floor for oil stains or other issues. This cleaning phase is included in all service packages. Deeper cleaning - pressure washing, oil stain treatment, full wall wash - is available as an add-on through our garage cleaning service. Calgary garages accumulate significant road salt, sand, and oil residue over a winter. Cleaning before installing shelving means the shelving contacts clean surfaces and adhesive-backed components (cable trays, label holders) actually stick. It also dramatically improves the feel of the finished space.

What Is Included vs. Not Included

Transparency is important to us. Here is a clear breakdown of what is and is not part of a standard GarageScape garage organization service in Calgary.

Always Included in Every Service

Pre-service consultation and written fixed-price quote. Two-person professional crew for the full service day. Complete removal and sorting of all garage contents. Decision-making session with homeowner on all ambiguous items. Donation transport to Calgary charities (no charge to homeowner). Floor sweep and basic wall cleaning of the empty garage. Organization of all kept items into labelled zones by category. Zone map document posted inside the garage door. Brief walkthrough with crew lead at end of service. Written 30-day workmanship guarantee on all installed hardware. GST invoice for the full service amount.

Included in Full Organization and Premium Packages

Installation of wall rail systems (Full Organization: one rail system, Premium: full wall coverage). Installation of steel shelving units (Full: up to four units, Premium: custom-designed full shelving plan). Installation of one overhead ceiling rack (Premium package only). Materials cost for shelving and rail systems is included in the Premium package price. In the Full Organization package, the customer provides or approves materials purchased by GarageScape at cost. Hockey gear station installation (hook rail and labelled sections). Tire wall bracket installation and setup. All anchoring hardware and installation supplies. Crew labour for the full installation including stud-finding, levelling, and load testing.

Not Included: Separate Services Available on Request

Deep floor cleaning or pressure washing (available as an add-on through our garage cleaning service). Hauling fees for large-item disposal beyond what fits in a standard donation run (quoted separately if needed). Flooring installation or epoxy floor coating (not a service we offer, but we can refer a Calgary flooring contractor). Electrical work, new outlet installation, or lighting upgrades (requires a licensed electrician). Garage door repairs, opener servicing, or weatherstripping (requires a garage door specialist). Moving items to other areas of the home beyond the garage. Storage of items off-site during or after the service. Painting of garage walls or ceiling. Custom-built cabinetry (we install pre-manufactured systems, not custom carpentry).

Popular Add-On Services

The following add-ons can be booked alongside any service package and will be included in the same-day service. Add Garage Deep Clean for a full pressure wash, oil stain treatment, and wall wash before we install the organization system. Add a Custom Storage Consultation for a professional storage layout design if you want to purchase materials yourself and install later. Add a spring or fall seasonal swap service where we rotate items between zones and do a maintenance purge on your behalf - priced separately as a shorter visit than a full organization service. All add-ons must be quoted and confirmed before service day. Same-day additions are accommodated when feasible but may extend the service completion time.

Frequently Asked Service Questions

Detailed answers to the questions Calgary customers ask most before booking. If you have a question not covered here, call us at (587) 804-9266 and we will answer it directly.

Q

Can you match an existing shelving system already in my garage?

Yes in most cases. If you have a brand-name modular shelving system already installed (Gladiator, Rubbermaid, Muscle Rack, etc.) and want to extend it or add components, we can source matching units and integrate them with the existing installation. If you have custom-built or unknown-brand shelving, we assess whether extending it makes sense structurally or whether replacing it with a compatible system is the better value. During the consultation, bring any installation manuals or photos you have of the existing shelving - this speeds up the compatibility check significantly.

Q

Do I need to buy the shelving before you arrive, or do you supply it?

In the Basic Sort package, we do not install new shelving, so no purchase is needed. In the Full Organization package, we can supply and purchase materials at cost on your behalf, or you can purchase them once we tell you exactly what to buy after the consultation. In the Premium package, all materials are included in the quoted price and we handle all purchasing. We recommend letting us handle the purchasing in the Full package as well - we buy only what fits and know which Calgary suppliers carry the specific products that hold up in Alberta's climate. Buying shelving before a consultation almost always results in the wrong products for the space.

Q

What happens if it rains on service day during the sort phase?

Light rain slows the driveway sort phase but does not stop it - we have tarps and can stage items in a covered area or inside the garage in sections when weather requires it. Heavy rain or hail requires rescheduling the sort phase. If this happens, we contact you first thing the morning of service and rebook the earliest available date. We do not start a sort phase in weather conditions that risk damaging your belongings. In Calgary, unexpected rain days in summer are occasionally an issue. We monitor the forecast before every service day and call ahead if conditions look problematic.

Q

Can I book a service for a condo or townhouse garage in Calgary?

Yes. Condo and townhouse garages in Calgary are some of the most challenging to organize due to their smaller footprint and the tendency for residents to store items for multiple units. We work in underground parkades (with stall-only organization), tandem garages, and small single-car attached garages common in inner-city Calgary condos. The same packages apply, with pricing adjusted for the smaller footprint. Some strata corporations require advance notice before a service is performed in common areas - if your condo has rules about this, let us know at booking and we will provide whatever documentation your strata needs.

Q

Do you offer repeat service or maintenance plans?

Yes. We offer a seasonal maintenance service where we return twice a year (typically April and October) to do the seasonal swap, a quick purge pass, and any minor reorganization needed. This is priced separately from the full organization service and is significantly less expensive - it is designed for garages that are already well organized and just need a periodic reset rather than a full rebuild. Ask about our maintenance plan pricing when you book your initial service. Many Calgary customers find that a twice-yearly maintenance visit, at roughly half the cost of a full service, keeps their garage in excellent condition indefinitely. Read more on our main organization page or the maintenance section of our complete guide.
