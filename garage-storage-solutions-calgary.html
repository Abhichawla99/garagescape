Frequently Asked Questions About Garage Storage Solutions in Calgary

What materials do you use for storage systems?

We use high-quality materials including heavy-duty steel, reinforced wood, and industrial-grade hardware. All materials are chosen for durability and strength in Calgary's climate.

Can you install storage around existing items?

Yes, we can work around existing items and design storage systems that accommodate your current setup while maximizing available space.

Do you provide a warranty on storage systems?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive warranty on all our custom storage systems, covering both materials and installation workmanship.

Can the storage be moved or reconfigured later?

Many of our storage systems are designed to be modular and can be reconfigured as your needs change. We'll discuss your future needs during the design phase.

For most residential storage installations, permits are not required. However, we'll handle any necessary permits for larger or more complex installations.
